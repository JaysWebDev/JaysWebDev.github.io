Methodology

How the Scoring Works

01
Public Data, Normalized
MassGIS parcel data and town assessor records cover every multifamily building in the state — 339,856 parcels with address, unit count, year built, and assessed value.
02
HUD FMR Yield Estimate
HUD Fair Market Rents for FY2025 are applied by bedroom count and county. Gross yield = annual FMR ÷ assessed value. A proxy, not a guarantee — but consistent across 339k buildings.
03
Composite Deal Score
Cap rate, distress signals (building age, ownership flags, permit gaps), and data quality confidence are combined into a 0–100 score. Sort by score to find the top of the stack fast.
