📋 Sophisticated Range Analysis:

Institutional-grade pre-breakout detection using multi-timeframe range compression, volume pattern analysis, and 52-week position metrics. Identifies coiling patterns before explosive moves.

🔍 Advanced Algorithm (70+ Indicators):

  • Gap Analysis + Bollinger Band Position
🎯 Position Metrics (15 pts):
  • 52-Week High Distance (>80% = 15 pts)

🎯 Setup Scanner (Anticipatory Pattern Detection)

📋 Strategy:

Detects stocks coiling for breakouts BEFORE the move happens. Three distinct setup types identify different pre-breakout mechanics using MACD, ADX, Bollinger Bands, OBV divergence, and trend structure analysis.

🔍 Three Setup Types:
🔥 Volatility Contraction Breakout (VCB):
  • Bollinger Band squeeze (width contracting)
  • Narrow Range (NR3/NR4) candle patterns
  • Low ADX (<25) = energy building
  • MACD histogram shifting positive
📦 Accumulation Breakout (ACCUM):
  • OBV divergence (volume rising, price flat)
  • Buying volume ratio > 1.2x selling
  • Near 52-week high (within 15%)
  • Minervini trend template alignment
📉 Pullback in Trend (PULLBACK):
  • Strong ADX trend (>22) confirming momentum
  • RSI pullback to 38-58 support zone
  • Price at SMA20 support level
  • Close > SMA50 > SMA150 trend structure
